Community

We partner with local and national organizations to keep all pets healthy and happy.

Mending Hearts Rescue

Mending Hearts Rescue is a not-for-profit animal organization dedicated to finding permanent homes for homeless animals through a foster based network of volunteers in the greater Rockford area.

Winnebago County Animal Services

Winnebago County offers financial assistance toward sterilization services for pets whose owners meet certain financial criteria. Please click the link above for more information.

Fresh Start Animal Rescue

Fresh Start Animal Rescue is a licensed no-kill rescue located in Belvidere, IL. For hours and adoption information, visit their homepage by clicking the link above.

Kibble Korner

Kibble Korner helps families who are struggling to keep their pets and families intact, by temporarily supplying food for their hungry pets in order to keep them in the homes they know and love.
