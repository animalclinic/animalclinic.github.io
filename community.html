Community

We partner with local and national organizations to keep all pets healthy and happy.

Mending Hearts Rescue

Mending Hearts Rescue is a not-for-profit animal organization dedicated to finding permanent homes for homeless animals through a foster based network of volunteers in the greater Rockford area.

Winnebago County

Winnebago County Animal Services offers financial assistance toward sterilization services for pets whose owners meet certain financial criteria. Click above for more information.

PawProject.org

The Paw Projectâ€™s mission is to educate the public about the painful and crippling effects of feline declawing, to promote animal welfare through the abolition of the practice of declaw surgery, and to rehabilitate cats that have been declawed.

Kibble Korner

Kibble Korner helps families who are struggling to keep their pets and families intact, by temporarily supplying food for their hungry pets in order to keep them in the homes they know and love.

SmartTag

SmartTag is the leading provider of pet protection & identification products in North America. SmartTag is the only pet ID protection system that has been sold in both PetCo and PetSmart nationally.

PetLink

PetLink is a pet recovery system which utilizes a microship that can be searched online to help pet owners find and bring their lost pets home safely. Lifetime registration and no annual fees to use this service.

SpayUSA

North Shore Animal League America's SpayUSA is a nationwide network and referral service for affordable spay/neuter services. Visit their website to learn how to obtain a certificate for discounted surgery.

All Paws Pet Cremation

We understand the end of a pet's life can be difficult, which is why we work with All Paws Pet Cremations to make that already difficult time a little easier knowing your pet is in loving hands from the moment they depart this earth.
